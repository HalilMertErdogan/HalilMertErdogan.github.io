Biography

Archaeology grounded in fieldwork, architecture and historical interpretation.

Halil Mert Erdoğan is an Assistant Professor of Archaeology and Deputy Director of the Olympos Excavations. His academic work engages with the archaeology of Lycia and Anatolia, with particular attention to ancient architecture, religious practices and cultural heritage.

His research is shaped by long-term field experience, architectural documentation, material analysis and publication. His work spans the Archaic, Classical, Hellenistic, Roman and Late Antique periods.
