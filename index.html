Profile

Research grounded in the field

Dr. Halil Mert Erdoğan is an archaeologist and academic whose work integrates archaeological excavation, architectural analysis, digital documentation, conservation practice and scientific publication.

His research focuses on the archaeology and architectural history of Lycia, with particular attention to ancient fortifications, religious architecture, sculptural contexts and the transformation of urban landscapes from the Hellenistic through the Roman periods.

He currently serves as Deputy Director of the Olympos Excavations and has participated in long-term field projects at Xanthos, Rhodiapolis, Beydağları and Prokonnesos.
